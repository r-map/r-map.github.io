4 @verbatim
5 The LTC®4015 is a complete synchronous buck controller/charger with pin
6 selectable, chemistry specific charging and termination algorithms. The LTC4015
7 can charge Li-Ion/Polymer, LiFePO4, or leadacid batteries. Battery charge
8 voltage is pin selectable and I²C adjustable. Input current limit and charge

11 monitors all system power parameters. Safety timer and current termination
12 algorithms are supported for lithium chemistry batteries. The LTC4015 also
13 includes automatic recharge, precharge (Li-Ion) and NTC thermistor protection.
14 The LTC4015's I²C port allows user customization of charger algorithms, reading
15 of charger status information, configuration of the maskable and programmable
16 alerts, plus use and configuration of the Coulomb counter. Available in a
