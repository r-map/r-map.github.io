Detailed Description

THERMAL_SHUTDOWN_BF Bit Field.

Indicates die temperature is greater than thermal shutdown level (160C typical)

  • Register: SYSTEM_STATUS
  • CommandCode: 57
  • Size: 1
  • Offset: 4
  • MSB: 4
  • MASK: 0x0010
  • Access: R
  • Default: n/a
